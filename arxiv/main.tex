\begin{abstract}
This paper introduces the OEA (Ontology, Epistemic, Agentic) Framework as a stability-oriented architecture for agentic systems. We evaluate whether ontological grounding and epistemic filtering improve recursive robustness against model collapse under iterative self-conditioning.
\end{abstract}
