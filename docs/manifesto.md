# The Architecture of Meaning
We are witnessing the entropy of digital thought. Recursive systems that consume synthetic outputs without ontological anchoring drift toward semantic collapse. The OEA Framework is proposed as an explicit corrective:

- **Ontology:** constrain output to first principles and meaningful jurisdiction.
- **Epistemic:** enforce falsification, calibration, and trust-aware grounding.
- **Agentic:** close the loop with experimental action and empirical feedback.

The thesis is operational, not rhetorical: model stability is improved when agent outputs remain tethered to philosophical and empirical constraints.
